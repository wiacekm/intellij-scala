Reports the definitions with an operator-like (symbolic) names without @targetName annotation.

It is recommended that definitions with symbolic names have a @targetName annotation. This will establish an alternate name that is easier to search for and will avoid cryptic encodings in runtime diagnostics.

Definitions with names in backticks that are not legal host platform names should also have a @targetName annotation.

See The @targetName annotation.

The quick-fix adds a @targetName annotation with an empty external name.
